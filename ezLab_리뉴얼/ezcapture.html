Is scroll capture possible?
Yes, you can capture a long vertical area that's hidden by a scroll bar all at once.
1. Select Scroll Capture.
2. Move your mouse to the scrolling area you want to capture; it will be selected automatically.
3. Click your mouse on the selected area, and the scroll will automatically move down from the top, capturing the entire section.
